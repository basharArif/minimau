Robodogs

Leafloat Robodogs are intelligent, quadruped robots engineered for high-mobility environments. With real-time navigation, obstacle detection, and remote control, they serve in inspection, public safety, and disaster response. Their all-terrain agility and AI-driven autonomy make them ideal for industrial applications.
